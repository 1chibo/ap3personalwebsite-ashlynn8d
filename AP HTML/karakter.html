ps. this isn't what kirby looks like in game

Kirby is one of my favorite characters. I like kirby because hes cute and hes actually more powerful than he looks in game. Kirby can inhale almost any object and completely control it to his will. Not only objects, but kirby can also inhale enemies and copy their abilities. I like the fact that he might not look as powerful than he actually is in game. Some people who saw him for the first time probably won't expect that kirby isn't from a cozy social simulation game like animal crossing, but rather from an action platformer.
